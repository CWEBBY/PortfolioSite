Your Local Air Conditioning Specialists

Anything Air & Refrigeration is a locally owned business specialising in providing prompt, personal service in home and commercial air conditioning.

We offer installation, maintenance, servicing & repairs across all brands, and ensure that your heating and cooling needs are met by measuring and assessing your individual needs, and providing a genuine, friendly service you can trust.

Anything Air is a fully licensed and insured local business who care for the comfort of your family home or workplace.

When you choose Anything Air, you can be assured of quality service and expert advice in a timely and professional manner.

Residential

Keeping your home cool is easier, and probably cheaper than you think!

There are so many options when it comes to heating or cooling your home and each will suit a slightly different space, that's why it pays to make sure you're getting the best for your home.

We take the time to assess your space and your needs, whether you're looking to cool a room or your entire home. This ensures that you get the best results for the best possible price immediately and in the longer term.

Commercial

A comfortable work environment makes a more productive business!

Many studies have shown that a cool, air conditioned work place can help to facilitiate more productive, focused employees. Just as important as installing a high quality unit is maintaining it.

Simply having your office's air conditioning unit cleaned, serviced and maintained once per year can help to take care of the health and productivity of your team, and extend the life of your unit.

Services

Anything Air is fully qualified and licensed to perform work on all brands of air conditioning units. We pride ourselves on providing quality units and workmanship, taking into consideration the unique requirements of each job.


Installation

To ensure you get the best unit for your space, we measure, calculate your requirements, and match you with a high quality unit from a reputable brand that will work efficiently to meet your needs.

Maintenance

To prolong the life and efficiency of your unit, we recommend regular maintenance. This can help to reduce the need for costly repairs or replacements before they are necessary.

Servicing

Regular servicing should be a part of your air conditioning maintenance routine. Once per year, a qualified technician can provide a full service to ensure the air you breathe is clean and your unit runs efficiently.

Repairs

Sometimes things break, but that doesn't always mean they need a replacement. In many cases, where a unit is not at the end of it's lifespan, repairs can save you money and extend its useful life.
